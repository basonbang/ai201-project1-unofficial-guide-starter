**Thread** 2024-05-30 — Full review: Tyler Devlin (Fall 2023)
> **leavism:** Just finished 665 with Tyler Devlin, here was my general experience:
> - Math heavy (of course, AI in general is math heavy).
> - Has two midterms and a final, along with 5-6 homework assignments.
> - Class average for the two midterms were ~45% and he curves heavily (A was 70%+, B was 50%+, C was 40%+, all subject to change based on future class averages).
> - Very difficult, but only one person failed his class Fall 2023.
> - Attendance not required.
> - Would recommend.
>
> From talking with friends, Tyler Devlin is harder than Akila's class (I believe Akila's midterm averages were 80%, but don't quote me on that), but Tyler curves his grades and Akila does not. Tyler is a great and fun lecturer, his office hours are invaluable, and he's a rather interesting person. I recommend you get to know him because he has industry experience and his hobbies are cool too. I was struggling through every subject but I didn't really mind. I thoroughly studied for the materials I found interesting and that was enough for a B on both midterms. I learned a lot and would recommend Tyler for anyone who's genuinely interested in AI.
