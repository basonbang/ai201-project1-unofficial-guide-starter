**Thread** 2021-05-25
> hey 615 folks, anyone care to share their experience for the class this semester? seriously contemplating about taking it. thanks!
  → Absolutely loved it! You gotta be good at looking stuff up on your own so if you got that you'll be good. It's all in C. Projects are really fun.
  → First assignment is a basic traffic light with LEDs, second is a tapeless ruler using an echo sensor, third is programming a motor/wheel to spin, assignment 4 is calculating speed of the wheel, assignment 5 is using infrared/line sensors, and after that you build the self driving car.
  → yes! super helpful! one more question: how much time are you expected to put into the assignments?
  → Hmm I don't know because it depends on the person, their familiarity with C, etc. There was one assignment that was super brutal and I put around 40 hours into it, but that was because there was zero references/tutorials online and not even the professor had done it in that same exact configuration, so there was a lot of trial and error. Aside from that one I think I put maybe 1-4 hours into the others.
  → No prob, I can't recommend that class enough. Hands down my favorite CS class I've ever taken.
