> @sweet_bee Personally, i thought it was an easy A class. A typical week goes like this: 
> - Every week you're given a section of the book to read and sometimes videos to watch
> - You write a response to the prompt he gives (500 - 700 words) using the ideas presented in the readings/videos in the forum
> - Reply to another person's response (300 - 400 words) in the forum
  → For the final, you have 3 choices: 
     1) Research report (600 - 900 words) using a current news article that talks about one of the course topics 
     2) 10 minute video covering a topic from the class
     3) Creative interpretation: Interpret a topic from this class through a creative art (painting, poetry, song, dance etc.) + 300 word explanation of relationship between your interpretation and a course topic
  → You can get through a lot of the word count by just restating the ideas in the readings/videos
