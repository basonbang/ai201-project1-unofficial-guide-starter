**Thread** [2021-05-10] — Course Review
> Since I don't see many reviews about multiplayer game development with Dr. Yoon I guess I'll take the time to write a review. This class is very learn-on-your-own and team-based, similar to CSC 648. You get to pitch your own game idea or work on a previously worked-on project from last semester. It's a heavy workload as you have to work on 4 homework assignments while at the same time work on your main project. There are no exams and it's not the best organized/structured class. Expect to present for every milestone and a final presentation where others will test your game.
  → (reply, Nov 2022) Hmm, this structure is terrible for me when it comes to learning.
  → (reply, Nov 2022) I actually really like it — you gotta have teammates who are dedicated to it. It makes you feel like "wow I have so much time" and then suddenly it's crunch time.
